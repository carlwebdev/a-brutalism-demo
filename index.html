BRUTALIST MANIFESTO

Documenting the non-negotiables that guide every experiment we ship.

  1. Amplify tension between order and chaos to make users feel something.
  2. Expose the grid, the scaffolding, and the seams—transparency is the new polish.
  3. Celebrate system glitches as signature moves, not bugs to be hidden.

Updated whenever the collective evolves a new instinct.
